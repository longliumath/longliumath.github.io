Research Interests 研究方向

My research interests lie in number theory and arithmetic geometry. Specifically, I work on rational points on algebraic varieties, abelian varieties, motives, zeta and L-functions, and related cohomology theories.

我的研究领域是数论与算术几何，涉及代数簇上的有理点、阿贝尔簇、motives、zeta与L-函数以及相关的上同调理论。
