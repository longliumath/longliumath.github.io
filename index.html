About Me

I am currently a postdoc at Institute for Theoretical Sciences of Westlake University. I am in the research group of Huayi Chen (陈华一).

Previously, I obtained my PhD degree from IMJ-PRG of Sorbonne Université (also known as Université Paris VI, Université Pierre-et-Marie-Curie, or even Université Jussieu).

Research Interests

Number theory and arithmetic geometry. In particular, raional points, abelian varieties, zeta and L-functions, Brauer groups, cohomology theories.
